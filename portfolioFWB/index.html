Hello, My Name is Fernando Brandao.


I was 18 years old when I first used a computer, an old IBM. Straight away it got my attention on how programming works.

After having earned my certificate as a Flight Attendant I decided I wanted a career change. I decided to become a Developer as I have always wanted to learn to code.

In October 2017, I joined the Development Professional Programme with Just IT. This course has allowed me to develop my basic knowledge and skills further which was the beginning of my journey to becoming a front-end developer. Now, my aim is to gain a developer role in London so that I can continue developing my technical skills and knowledge to become a Full Stack.

My passion for code gets bigger every day, I even created a blog using WordPress brand.fernandobrandao.co.uk to translate my goals, frustration, improvements and everything that will one day make me a great developer. Have a look if you have a moment.
